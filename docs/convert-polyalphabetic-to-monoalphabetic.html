Convert Polyalphabetic to Monoalphabetic

Convert a periodic polyalphabetic ciphertext to monoalphabetic form using a reconstruction matrix. Each ciphertext letter is mapped through its period-indexed alphabet row to the line-0 reference alphabet. Unknown positions (no entry in the matrix row) are shown as ..

Matrix format: one row per line, <name> <alphabet>. Line 0 is the reference plaintext alphabet (defaults to ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ if absent). Lines 1–N are the cipher alphabets; their count determines the period. Use . for unknown positions. Blank lines and lines starting with # are ignored.
